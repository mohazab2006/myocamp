# MYO Camp

MYO Camp is a volunteer-run Muslim youth summer camp for campers ages 9 to 16 and Leaders in Training ages 17 to 19. The site serves parents, youth, volunteers, donors, and community partners who need to understand the camp experience, location, safety standards, registration details, and ways to support the program.

The brand should feel outdoorsy, grounded, warm, capable, and community-held. The tone is clear and trusted for parents while still carrying the energy of a week outdoors: cabins, trails, canoe docks, campfires, prayer, friendship, leadership, and service.

Strategic priorities:
- Make registration and key logistics easy to find.
- Preserve the camp's Muslim youth identity without turning the site into institutional brochureware.
- Use real camp photography as the visual anchor.
- Build trust through safety, volunteer leadership, and practical details.
- Keep the experience polished enough for donors and community partners.

Anti-references:
- Cartoonish summer-camp clip art.
- Generic beige outdoors lifestyle branding.
- Dense static pages with repeated navigation ribbons.
- Template card grids with icons above every heading.

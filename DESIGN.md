# MYO Camp Design Direction

Register: brand.

Scene: a parent checks camp details in the evening while a camper scrolls the photos beside them, both wanting the place to feel real, safe, and exciting.

Color strategy: full palette with tinted natural neutrals, forest green, pine shadow, lake blue, ember, brass, and weathered paper. Avoid pure black and pure white.

Typography: Geist Sans for clean, durable reading and strong display scale. Avoid playful novelty fonts for the new site.

Layout:
- Cinematic first viewport with real camp photography and a restrained floating navigation.
- Wide editorial sections with large type, but keep headings to 2 or 3 lines.
- Dense bento and staggered media compositions instead of identical cards.
- Registration and rules content should be structured for scanning.

Motion:
- GSAP entrance choreography, scroll-triggered image scaling, pinned camp-life section on desktop, and scrubbed text reveals.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion.

Components:
- Floating nav with anchored links.
- High-contrast CTAs.
- Bento program overview.
- Horizontal accordion for camp spaces.
- Practical rules and packing lists.
- Embedded registration form.
